This is a program designed to evaluate your current level of knowledge of hanzi (Chinese characters) as used in Mandarin Chinese. You will be presented characters and be asked to select the correct pronunciation and meaning in English in a multiple choice format. At the end of the test, you will be given an estimate of the approximate amount of Chinese characters you know.

The goal of the test is to give you an idea of how many Chinese characters you know in order to help you evaluate your current level and guide your study goals. Test your Hanzi Knowledge

The goal of this test is to estimate the amount of hanzi you know. You will be presented with a hanzi prompt, and be asked to select the correct pronunciation and definition from four choices. If you do not know the answer, you can simply click the "I don't know" button. Submitting "I don't know" has the exact same effect as submitting an incorrect answer. You are encouraged, however, to not guess if you are unsure of the correct answer. Guessing or using process of elimination when you are unsure of the answer will result in a more inaccurate calculation of your knowledge.

The test should take 5-10 minutes. You must complete the entire test to see your results.

Results given are an estimation. If you would like a more accurate calculation of your knowledge, try taking the test multiple times and averaging your results.

About

This application was made with the goal to help you estimate how many Chinese characters (hanzi) you know. It uses the 6,000 most commonly used Chinese characters, as identified by the PRC Ministry of Education. Test prompts have been randomized, so you are guaranteed to have a unique experience every time you take the test. While the application's algorithm has been carefully designed to accurately assess your knowledge, the random nature of the test means your score may vary between evaluations. Take the test multiple times and average your results to get a more precise assessment.

Approximate skill levels:

    0-500 Characters: Beginner

    500-1000 Characters: Lower Intermediate

    1000-2000 Characters: Intermediate

    2000-3000 Characters: Upper Intermediate

    3000-4000 Characters: Advanced

    4000-5000 Characters: Highly Advanced

    5000-6000 Characters: Hanzi Hero

If you suspect that your results are not well-correlated with your skill level, please take a screenshot, and send me a message with the details of the situation.
